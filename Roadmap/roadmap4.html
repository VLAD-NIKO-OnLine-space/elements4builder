Roadmap

Milestones on the way

A simple timeline that shows the most important checkpoints the project passes through before it feels complete.

01

Kickoff

Collect requirements and patterns

Review what each site in the network should contain and decide which repeating fragments will become reusable sections.

02

Design

Shape layouts and hierarchy

Align typography, spacing, and hierarchy so every page looks consistent, even when sections are swapped or reordered.

03

Automation

Wire content to the engine

Connect JSON manifests, section definitions, and image folders so new sites are generated with a single command.

04

Launch

Deploy, measure, adjust

Launch generated sites, review live output, and refine details while keeping the core system stable.
